Investor Information

TerraNova GreenBuild is currently in a strategic growth phase, presenting a valuable opportunity for investors who are interested in sustainable innovation, environmental responsibility, and long-term financial returns. The company operates within the construction industry while integrating modern green technologies, positioning itself as a future-focused and impactful enterprise.

We are seeking investors who are aligned not only with financial growth, but also with a deeper vision of transforming the construction industry into a force for environmental restoration and social progress. Investment capital will be used to expand operations, acquire advanced equipment, develop infrastructure, and train skilled professionals who will drive innovation and excellence.

By investing in TerraNova GreenBuild, stakeholders become part of a long-term vision that extends beyond profit. They contribute to building a future where architecture supports environmental balance, economic sustainability, and improved quality of life for communities.

Why Invest in TerraNova GreenBuild?

  • Growing Demand for Green Buildings: The demand for eco-friendly construction is rapidly increasing both locally and globally. Climate awareness and sustainability policies are driving individuals, businesses, and governments toward greener solutions, creating a strong and expanding market.
  • Strong and Scalable Business Model: TerraNova operates through diversified revenue streams, including construction services, consultation, and renewable system installations. This reduces risk while allowing scalable growth from local operations to international markets.
  • Long-Term Cost Efficiency: Our building designs significantly reduce energy consumption, water usage, and maintenance costs. This provides long-term financial value to clients, increasing demand and customer satisfaction.
  • Environmental and Social Impact: Investment directly contributes to reducing environmental damage, conserving natural resources, and improving living conditions. Additionally, the company promotes job creation and skills development within communities.
  • First-Mover Advantage: Sustainable construction is still developing in Lesotho and surrounding regions. TerraNova has the opportunity to establish itself as a leading and trusted brand before the market becomes highly competitive.

Funding Requirements

The company is seeking funding to support the following key areas:
