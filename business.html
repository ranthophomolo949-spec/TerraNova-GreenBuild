Revenue Streams

Construction Service Fees

TerraNova GreenBuild generates income by providing full eco-friendly construction services. Clients pay for the design and building of homes, offices, and other structures. Pricing depends on project size and sustainability features such as solar systems and water-saving technologies. This ensures steady revenue while delivering long-term value.

Consultation Fees

The company offers expert advice on sustainable building practices. Clients pay for services such as energy efficiency planning, site analysis, and eco-design recommendations. This allows income generation even without full construction projects.

Solar and Water System Installations

TerraNova installs renewable energy systems and water management solutions. Clients pay for both equipment and installation. These systems reduce long-term costs, making them attractive and profitable services.

Partnership Commissions

The company partners with suppliers and earns commissions by recommending or using their products. This creates additional income while ensuring high-quality materials.

Target Market

TerraNova GreenBuild targets homeowners, businesses, and government projects seeking sustainable construction solutions. Market potential is analyzed using TAM, SAM, and SOM.

Growth Plan

TerraNova will begin operations locally in Maseru, building a strong reputation through small projects. Expansion will follow across Lesotho, Southern Africa, and eventually global markets. Growth includes:

  • Training skilled workers
  • Adopting new eco-friendly technologies
  • Forming strategic partnerships
  • Expanding service offerings to energy, water, and waste management
  • Brand marketing and educational outreach

Competitive Advantage

Eco-Friendly Solutions: The company prioritizes sustainability through renewable energy and efficient materials.

Cost-Saving Designs: Buildings are designed to reduce long-term energy and maintenance costs.

Modern Construction Methods: Innovative techniques improve efficiency, durability, and construction speed.
